The NIFTY50 represents the benchmark for the Indian stock market. It is calculated through weighted average of top 50 Indian companies listed on the National Stock Exchange(NSE). Overall NIFTY50 index provides the broad picture about the Indian economy.

It includes the companies from several sectors like Financial services, Energy, IT, Consumer goods, Automobiles, and agriculture sector. Major companies are Indian Oil Corporation, Tata Steels, Infosys, Larsen & Turbo, State Bank of India, Sun Pharmaceutical etc.
